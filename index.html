About Me

Travel is My Passion

Hello! I'm Riya Verma. I believe every destination has a story waiting to be discovered. Travel isn't just about reaching beautiful places— it's about collecting unforgettable memories, meeting wonderful people, finding peace in nature, and discovering yourself.

My Kedarnath and Rishikesh journey became one of the most memorable experiences of my life. Through this blog, I share my travel stories, photography, helpful travel tips, and Hindi interpretations of meaningful English quotes that inspire me every day.

"Mountains are calling and I must go."

पहाड़ केवल घूमने की जगह नहीं, बल्कि मन को शांति देने वाला एहसास हैं।
